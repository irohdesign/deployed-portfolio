Biomedical Research Workforce Dashboard (BMW)
Featured Skill: Graphing

The Biomedical Research Workforce Dashboard displays data collected from National Institutes of Health employees, their education level, and their ethnicity. We parsed the data from dozens of Institute and Centers (IC) covering thousands of employees over 45 years. Our dashboard was built on Vue.js, with eCharts.js and D3.js as the graphing libraries.
